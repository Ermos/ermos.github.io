It's Nook

Pattern Editor for the video game Animal Crossing

Stack : Nuxt.js, Golang, Gitlab-ci, Docker

The idea behind this application was to bring back a breath of freshness in the world of pattern editors on Animal Crossing. Most being aging whether it is ergonomically or in terms of features, I decided to create my own editor.

It's Nook is therefore a mix between the existing pattern editors and digital drawing tools such as Paint or Photoshop, the interface is also strongly inspired by the latter.

This project allowed me to discover a lot of concept in the image processing, it is also one of my first highly interactive web interface.
